The Methodology

The 4-Phase Growth Cycle.

Every coaching session follows a structured pedagogical framework designed to maximize retention, identify blind spots, and build real-world confidence.

Phase 1

Context & Calibration

The coach reviews your recent progress, verifies your goals for the session, and asks a warm-up question based on your resume to establish a baseline.

Phase 2

Deep Dive Exploration

The LLM dynamically generates complex, multi-part scenarios. It listens to your response and actively probes your weak points to ensure deep understanding.

Phase 3

Behavioral Nuance

Shifting to soft skills, the AI coaches you on structuring your answers using the STAR method, focusing on leadership and effective communication.

Phase 4

Action Plan & Review

The session wraps up with immediate, constructive feedback. The AI provides specific action items and resources for you to study before the next session.

Built For Scale

Career-Focused Architecture.

Tenkir AI isn't just a chatbot wrapper—it's a massive orchestration of real-time technologies. We process audio chunks, map facial blendshapes, and stream LLM tokens simultaneously in the browser to achieve an incredibly lifelike coaching experience.

React Three Fiber & WebGL

Hardware-accelerated 3D rendering creates a pristine, distraction-free environment directly in the browser, enhancing focus during your sessions.

Web Audio API & ARKit

An Audio AnalyserNode maps raw streaming voice frequencies to 52 precise ARKit facial blendshapes for flawless, procedural lip-syncing.

Next.js & Supabase

A robust backend architecture ensures your progress data is saved securely, backed by strict Row-Level Security (RLS).

app/api/coach/route.ts 
// 1. Analyze session transcript
const feedback = await llm.evaluate({
  resume: professional.cvText,

// 2. Save progress securely
